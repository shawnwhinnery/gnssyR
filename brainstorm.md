- infinite RNG collecting for replayability. We need to determine how we make this game replayabler. Here are some succesful examples. We should identify the secret saus.
    - Elden Ring Nightreighn has randomized gems
        - players have 3 gem slots
        - each slot has a color and players can equip gems of the corrsponding color
        - each gem has 1 to 3 slots
        - the slots are populated randomly
        - players are awarded a small number of randomyl generated numbers at the end of each run
        - this brings players back over and over
    - Pokemon Go has randomized internal values "IVs"
        - pokemon each have individal random internal values that determine each pokemon's unique genetic limits.
        - players are not only collecting a variety of pokemon but they want to have a collection of high IV or "perfect" pokemon
        - players catch hundreds of the same pokemon over and over and over
    - Sonic has the Chao Garden
        - chao are little friendly animals who eat woodland creatures and take on their attributes
        - players play the same levels over and over agin in order to collect specific woodland critters 
        - those creatures are returned to the chao garden and selectively fed to the chao
        - the player retunrs to a level and completes it again in order to collect more woodland critters
